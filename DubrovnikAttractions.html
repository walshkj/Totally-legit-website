Attractions

Dubrovnik offers a wide range of activities throughout the entire city ranging from cany shops and beaches to stunningly scenic walks and rest stops overlooking the Adriatic Sea.

























The wall walk is one of the most famous parts of the city, known for its
vast scenery featuring magnifcent views of both the city and the Adriatic.





The main street or Stradun is a lively and picturesque walking area
that holds many unique and colorful shops as well as restaurants.
