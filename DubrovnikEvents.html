Events

The Croatian culture can be seen throuhgout the city on any normal day but there are some days where it can be seen everywhere.
Every year dubrovnik hosts events ranging from food and wine-tasting festivals to summer plays performed within the city walls.

























The Midsummer Scene is an Englsih theatre festival that has performs a Shakespeare play each Summer
It is performed on the main church square and is a unique and fascinating experience.





Dubrovnik hosts a yearly festival of "good food" where local vendors can share their creations and styles.
Wine-tasting is an integral part of the festival scene and Dubrovnik is known for being an international attraction for wine makers.
