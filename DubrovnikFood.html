Food and Dining

Although nearly all restaurants in the area are seafood-based, the food will not leave one disappointed.
There are plenty of unique spots throught the city and surrounding it that offer fantastic Croatian cuisine.

























Grilled fish, clams, and seafood platters are delicious local specialties. A lot of the foods are
cooked or grilled before the meat is even filleted and are served in the same way.





The restaurants themselves are unique and have exceptional style, especially within the city. Many are
incorporated into the desing of the surrounding architecture and can offer breathtaking views as well.
