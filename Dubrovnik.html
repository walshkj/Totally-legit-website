Dubrovnik


Dubrovnik is a historic city in Croatia that rests along the Adriatic Sea. It has a rich history stemming from its origins in the 7th century as a Greek settlement. It has grown as a popular tourist site and through conflict into the incredible destination and cultural site it is today.
























Restaurants are known for their scenic views and incorporation into
the design of the city as well as their Adriatic-style dining.







The main street or Stradun is a lively and picturesque walking area
that holds many unique and colorful shops as well as restaurants.
